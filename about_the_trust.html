ABOUT THE TRUST

The Sampriti Educational and Social Development Trust bearing the No.- IV-00233/09 Date:- 26-10-2009 (Serial No. 08257 of 2009) was formed for the purpose of providing quality education to the students, especially to the rural area student community. The Trust has completed a new building which would provide the best infrastructural facilities of the D.EL.Ed. (Diploma in Elementary Teacher Education Programme) College, namely Sampriti College of Education located at Vill & PO:- Hetampur, Dist:- Birbhum, West Bengal, Pin:-731124. The proposed College will be affiliated to the West Bengal Board of Primary Council (WBBPC) and recognised by the N.C.T.E. The College will be located ideal surrounding, attached to the Bus Stand, 4 km from Railway Station & adjacent to the National Highway- 60.

Vision & Mission :


1) To establish educational institute maintaining high academic standard in the field of professional education.

2) To provide trained Manpower to meet ever increasing demand of Educational, Industrial and serviced sector of the nation.

3) to do any activity which will be in the interest of public service
